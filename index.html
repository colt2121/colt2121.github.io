About me

My Past

I’ve built a foundation in design and development, with a growing interest in UI/UX design for the web and digital media. I’ve taken advanced computer graphics courses and two web design classes, where I learned the basics of creating user-friendly websites and working with visual elements. I’ve built a few websites on my own and recently completed an internship with Digital Retail Partners / Digital Sports Partners, where I helped create digital adverts for clients—mainly for social media and websites, as well as experimenting with some of the basics of designing different UI/UX elements of a page across my time in college. It was a great opportunity to get real-world experience and learn more about working in a creative environment.

My Present

As a recent community college graduate with a major in Graphic Design and a focus on UI/UX, I'm currently looking for new opportunities where I can continue to grow and challenge myself. To build on what I’ve already learned, I’m enrolled in a UI/UX Graphic Design bootcamp, where I’m gaining more hands-on experience with user-centered design, practical tools, and real-world projects. I'm excited to keep learning and take the next steps in my design journey.

My Future

After completing my graphic design bootcamp, I'm eager to find opportunities that will allow me to step outside my comfort zone and grow in new environments. I’m especially interested in expanding my skill set by learning more design tools and software, while also deepening my understanding of how design and code work together in a project. By improving both sides of the process, I hope to optimize my workflow and become a more versatile and effective designer.
